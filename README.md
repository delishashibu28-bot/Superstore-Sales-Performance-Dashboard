# Superstore-Sales-Performance-Dashboard


An interactive Tableau dashboard designed to analyze and visualize the sales performance, trends, and product distributions of a retail superstore.

##  Key Insights & Features

* **High-Level KPIs:** Tracked Total Sales ($241,131) and Total Orders (1,000) at a single glance.
* **Geographical Distribution:** An interactive map visualizing sales performance across different US states.
* **Product Performance:** Breakdowns of sales by Category (Furniture, Office Supplies, Technology) and their respective Sub-Categories to identify top-performing products.
* **Monthly Sales Trend:** A time-series line chart highlighting sales fluctuations throughout the year to pinpoint peak seasons.



##  Tools Used
* **Data Visualization:** Tableau Public
* **Data Source:** Superstore Dataset (Excel/CSV)
